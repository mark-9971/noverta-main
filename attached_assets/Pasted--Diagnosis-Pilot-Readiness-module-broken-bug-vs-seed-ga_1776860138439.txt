## Objective

Diagnose the broken Pilot Readiness module and determine whether the failure is caused by:
- missing or unrealistic seeded data
- broken product queries/logic
- stale assumptions in the module
- or some combination

## Why now

Pilot Readiness has been flagged as broken and may be one of the main reasons the current seed path feels insufficient.
Before seed rewrite begins, we need to know whether this module is broken because:
- the seeder under-populates its dependencies, or
- the module itself is wrong

## Scope

In scope:
- reproduce the broken Pilot Readiness behavior
- trace the page/module to the exact data sources it reads
- inspect the backing queries/routes/calculations
- compare expected data against what sample/demo seed currently populates
- classify the issue

Out of scope:
- no full fix implementation yet unless one tiny diagnostic fix is unavoidable
- no seed rewrite yet
- no broad module redesign
- no speculative roadmap work

## Required deliverables

By the end:
1. the module’s failure mode is concretely described
2. every major card/check/status on the Pilot Readiness surface is mapped to its backing data/query
3. the issue is classified as:
   - BUG
   - SEED GAP
   - BOTH
4. the recommended fix path is explicit and sequenced

## What I need you to inspect

For every visible Pilot Readiness indicator/check:
