Phase 2C-4: hide or support-gate internal / Trellis-only surfaces from customer-facing roles.

Goal:
Now that the core role navs and home surfaces are cleaner, remove remaining customer-facing exposure to internal, support-only, pilot-ops, debug, and Trellis-only pages. These routes can remain reachable, but they should not appear in navs or obvious customer-facing entry points for normal demo roles.

Why this matters:
A clean demo is not just about what is emphasized. It is also about what users cannot accidentally see. Internal pilot tooling, support/debug pages, and Trellis-ops surfaces make the product feel unfinished or operationally messy if they leak into customer-facing shells.

Hard constraints:

* Visibility / IA cleanup only
* No route deletions
* No backend changes
* No API changes
* No schema changes
* No auth/capability rewrite
* Keep routes reachable by direct URL if the app still depends on them
* Prefer nav/entry-point cleanup and support-role gating over invasive refactors
