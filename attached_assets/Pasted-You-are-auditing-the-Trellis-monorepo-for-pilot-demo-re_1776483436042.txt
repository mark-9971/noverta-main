# Audit standards

## Be evidence-based

Cite code locations, route files, pages, components, or config wherever possible.

## Do not accept surface polish as readiness

Something looking nice does not mean it is pilot-ready.

## Do not confuse “exists” with “works”

A route or feature only counts as strong if it appears operationally coherent and trustworthy.

## Be skeptical of breadth

If the app is trying to be too many things, say so clearly.

## Prefer truth over encouragement

Do not soften critical findings.
