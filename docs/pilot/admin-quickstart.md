# Trellis Admin Quickstart Guide

**For District Administrators**  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Date:** April 15, 2026

This guide covers the 10 most important things to do in your first week with Trellis.

---

## Before You Start

You need:
- Your Trellis admin login (email + password, or Google SSO if your district uses it)
- Your district's current IEP roster (a spreadsheet is fine)
- A list of staff to invite (name, email, role)

---

## 1. Confirm Your District Settings

After logging in, go to **Setup** (left sidebar, near the bottom).

Verify:
- Your district name is correct
- Your school(s) are listed
- Your service year dates are set

If anything is missing, email support before adding students.

---

## 2. Add Your Staff

Go to **Staff** → click **Add Staff Member**.

For each person, enter:
- First and last name
- Email address
- Role: choose from Admin, Case Manager, Coordinator, SPED Teacher, Provider, or Para

Each person will receive an email invitation to set up their login. They must sign in before you can assign them to students.

---

## 3. Add a Student

Go to **Students** → click **Add Student**.

Required fields:
- First and last name
- Date of birth
- Grade
- School
- Disability category

Optional but recommended:
- Parent/guardian name, email, and phone
- Primary language

Add students one at a time, or ask Trellis support about CSV import if you have more than 20 students.

---

## 4. Start an IEP for a Student

1. Go to **Students** and click the student's name.
2. Click **IEP** in the top tabs.
3. Click **New IEP Document**.
4. Enter the IEP start date and end date (annual review date).
5. Add service requirements: click **Add Service**, choose the service type (e.g., "Speech-Language Therapy"), and enter the mandated minutes per week.
6. Save.

The student now appears on the compliance calendar and will generate alerts when the annual review is approaching.

---

## 5. Log a Session

When a service session happens:

1. Go to **Sessions** → click **Log Session**.
2. Select the student, service type, and date.
3. Enter the duration in minutes.
4. Set status: **Completed** or **Missed**.
5. Save.

Case managers and providers can log their own sessions. Admins can log sessions for any student.

---

## 6. Log a Restraint or Seclusion Incident

Do this as soon as possible — ideally within 2 hours of the incident.

1. Go to **Protective Measures** (left sidebar).
2. Click **New Incident**.
3. Fill in: student, date, time, type of restraint or seclusion, duration, injury status.
4. Record whether verbal parent notification was made within 24 hours.
5. Save and set status to **Under Review**.

The system will track whether a written report has been sent and whether DESE notification is required.

---

## 7. Invite a Parent

**During the pilot period**, parents do not have their own login to Trellis. The parent invitation and communication workflow is:

**Step 1 — Add the parent's contact information to the student record:**
1. Open the student's profile → **Edit Student**.
2. Fill in: Parent/Guardian Name, Parent Email, and Parent Phone.
3. Save.

**Step 2 — Log all parent contacts as they happen:**
1. Open the student's profile → **Contacts** tab.
2. Click **Log Contact**.
3. Fill in: date, contact type (phone call / email / meeting), subject, and outcome.

This manual log is the IDEA/603 CMR 28.07 compliant method for tracking parent notification and consent during the pilot. Use it to document verbal notifications for incidents, IEP meeting notices, and consent confirmations.

**After the pilot:** A parent portal with direct login and document sharing is planned for release. When it becomes available, parents added to student records during the pilot will be the first to receive portal invitations.

---

## 8. Run a Compliance Report

Go to **Reports**.

- **Executive Summary** — district-wide IEP and service compliance overview
- **Compliance Trend** — month-over-month compliance rate chart
- **At-Risk** — students with overdue IEPs or missed service minutes
- **Exports** (admins and case managers only) — download CSV files for DESE reporting or internal audits

Run the **At-Risk** tab weekly. Any student flagged there needs attention before the end of the month.

---

## 9. Check the Alerts Feed

Go to **Alerts** (bell icon or left sidebar).

Alerts are automatically generated for:
- IEPs due for annual review within 30 days
- IEPs that are overdue
- Students whose service minutes are below mandate

Review alerts at least twice a week. Click any alert to go directly to the relevant student record.

---

## 10. When Something Goes Wrong

**If you can't log in:** Try a password reset from the sign-in page. If that doesn't work, email support.

**If data looks wrong:** Do not delete anything. Take a screenshot and email support with the student ID and a description.

**If the system is down:** Check your email — we send outage notifications to district admins within 30 minutes. The support email is your fastest path to an update.

**Support email:** support@trellis.education  
**Subject format:** `[Your District] P1/P2/P3 — brief description`

See the full [Support Process](./support-process.md) for response time expectations.
