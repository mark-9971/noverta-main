# Noverta Pilot Onboarding Checklist

**For District Administrators**  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Date:** April 15, 2026

Complete these steps before allowing case managers and teachers to begin entering data. Each step is linked to the full instructions in the [Admin Quickstart Guide](./admin-quickstart.md).

---

## Pre-Go-Live Checklist

### Step 1 — System Access
- [ ] District admin account is created and confirmed
- [ ] Admin can log in to Noverta successfully
- [ ] District name and school(s) are correct in Setup

### Step 2 — Staff Accounts
- [ ] All case managers have been added and have received their invitation email
- [ ] All case managers have logged in and confirmed their accounts
- [ ] Roles are assigned correctly (Case Manager, Coordinator, Teacher, Provider, Para)

### Step 3 — Student Roster
- [ ] All active IEP students have been entered in the Students section
- [ ] Each student has a school, grade, and disability category assigned
- [ ] Parent/guardian name and contact information is entered for each student

### Step 4 — IEP Records
- [ ] Each active student has at least one IEP Document created
- [ ] IEP start date and annual review (end) date are correct for each student
- [ ] At least one service requirement is added per student (service type + mandated minutes/week)

### Step 5 — Compliance Check
- [ ] Open Reports → At-Risk and confirm no students appear as overdue on day one (if any appear, verify their IEP dates)
- [ ] Open Reports → Executive Summary and confirm student count matches expected roster

### Step 6 — Incident Reporting Readiness
- [ ] Protective Measures page is accessible to the relevant staff
- [ ] Staff who oversee restraint/seclusion reporting know how to log an incident
- [ ] The verbal notification field and written report tracking are understood by responsible staff

### Step 7 — Staff Orientation
- [ ] Each case manager has completed a 15-minute walkthrough of: logging a session, viewing compliance status, and using the alerts feed
- [ ] The [Support Process](./support-process.md) email address has been shared with all staff

---
